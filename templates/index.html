Single Merchant Payment Qr code

Single Payment QR

A Single Merchant UPI Payment QR Code is a specific type of Quick Response (QR) code designed for facilitating seamless and secure transactions between a customer and a single merchant using the Unified Payments Interface (UPI) system. UPI is a real-time payment system in India that allows users to link multiple bank accounts to a single mobile application, making transactions easier and more convenient.

How to use the Single Payment Qr

❁ Enter the Vpa or Upi Id (Provided by your Bank)
❁ Enter the Your Company Name
❁ Enter Your Name
❁ Enter your Email Address
❁ Then Click Generate
